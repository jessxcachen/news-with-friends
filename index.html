Read An Article

Our chatbot sends articles for you through Facebook Messenger

Everyday the News With Friends chatbot will send you and your friends the top article of the day, but there is a twist. Each user will get the same topic but be presented an article from a different news source. Also, the user will be presented with a very minimalist UI with just the article and the main images. We remove the new source in the beginning to stop any pre-bias from occuring.

React To An Article

React to the news with six different Facebook emojis.

Show your friends how you feel about the topic and the article you are reading by reacting to it! Use the familiar Facebook reactions to show how much you love or hate a particular phrase, quote, sentance or paragraph. Each reaction will be saved and presented to you and your friends at the end of the day to facilitate better discussions!

Discuss About The Topic

Discuss friends' reactions to get a diverse perspective.

After you and all your friends finish reading and reacting to the article of the day, the News With Friends chatbot will send you all detailed statistics about everyone's reactions. There will be three ways to view the reactions: Total number of each reaction, Selected text by reaction and Selected text by person. Everyone is encouraged to read through all the stats to get a summary of all your friends' articles and see diverse point of views. These stats allow you and your friends to have more detailed and engaging dicussions online!

News With Friends

Read, react, & discuss!

News With Friends is a chatbot and web integration application that allows users to get exposed to diverse political news perspectives, where each member of the group gets to read the same news topic of a different political perspective. Users are able to engage by reacting to the news article with various reactions (like, love, angry, etc) and see the statistics of their friends reactions afterwards. This will promote news discussions with their friends and understand varying perspectives.
